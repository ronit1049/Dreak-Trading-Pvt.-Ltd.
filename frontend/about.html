About Liventra

Crafting beautiful furniture that turns houses into homes. Trusted by thousands across India.

Our Story

Liventra Furniture & Decor began as a small workshop with a big dream — to create furniture that blends comfort, elegance, and durability. Today, we serve thousands of homes and offices with thoughtfully designed products.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, stylish, and functional furniture that enhances everyday living.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted furniture brand known for innovation and craftsmanship.

Our Manufacturing Process

1. Design

Creative concepts crafted by expert designers.

2. Material Selection

Premium wood and fabrics sourced responsibly.

3. Crafting

Handcrafted by skilled artisans with precision.

4. Finishing

Polishing and detailing for a premium look.

Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes strict quality checks to ensure durability, safety, and comfort. We stand behind our furniture with warranties and customer-first service.
